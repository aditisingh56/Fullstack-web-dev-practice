Welcome to Punjab

Punjab, the "Land of Five Rivers", is a culturally rich and agriculturally vibrant state in northern India. Known for its warm hospitality, colorful traditions, historic landmarks, and mouth-watering cuisine, Punjab holds a unique place in the heart of India.

Culture & Traditions of Punjab

Punjab's culture is vibrant, colorful, and deeply rooted in music, dance, food, and festivals. From the rhythmic beats of Bhangra to spiritual hymns of Gurbani, Punjab's cultural heartbeat is truly captivating.

Bhangra Dance

A joyful folk dance performed during harvest festivals and celebrations.

Giddha Dance

A lively traditional dance performed by Punjabi women with graceful moves and rhythmic claps.

Punjabi Cuisine

Experience the taste of authentic dishes like Sarson da Saag, Makki di Roti, and Lassi.

Lohri Festival

Lohri is celebrated in winter to mark the harvest season with bonfires, sweets, and dancing.
