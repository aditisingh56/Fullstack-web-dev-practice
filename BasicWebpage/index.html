Punjab Rivers

Punjab is a state in northern India, known as the Land of Five Rivers. Its capital is Chandigarh, and it shares borders with Pakistan and several Indian states. Famous for its rich culture, agriculture, and vibrant festivals, Punjab is often called the "Granary of India."
