Top Tourist Attractions in Punjab

Explore the most iconic places that define Punjab's cultural and spiritual heritage.

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The holiest Sikh shrine, known for its stunning golden architecture and spiritual ambiance.

Jallianwala Bagh

Historic public garden and memorial of the 1919 massacre during British rule.

Wagah Border

Witness the iconic Beating Retreat ceremony between India and Pakistan every evening.

Anandpur Sahib

A sacred city where Guru Gobind Singh founded the Khalsa Panth. Home to Virasat-e-Khalsa.

Sheesh Mahal, Patiala

Beautiful palace of mirrors built by Maharaja Narinder Singh, now a museum.

Rock Garden, Chandigarh

A unique sculpture garden created from waste materials by Nek Chand.

Virasat-e-Khalsa

A stunning museum celebrating Sikh history and heritage, located in Anandpur Sahib.

Qila Mubarak, Bathinda

One of the oldest forts in India where Razia Sultan was imprisoned.
